About Us

Lasaco Trading and Investment Company Limited is a financial services company based in Lagos. It is involved in the trading and investment of a variety of financial products, including stocks, bonds, commodities, and other assets. we offer a range of services to individual and institutional clients, including financial planning, portfolio management, market analysis, and more. We aims to provide our clients with a comprehensive suite of financial services to help them achieve their investment goals. The company leverages its expertise in the Nigerian financial markets and its understanding of local economic conditions to make informed investment decisions and generate profits for its clients.
